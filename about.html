About pikCarz

Built on trust, powered by innovation — we're changing the way South Africa buys and sells vehicles.

Our Story

South Africa's Vehicle Marketplace, Reimagined

pikCarz was born from a simple idea: buying and selling a vehicle in South Africa should be easy, transparent, and trustworthy. We built a platform where private sellers and professional dealers can connect with serious buyers — all in one place.

Powered by Cube Absolute Services (Cubeas), pikCarz brings together cutting-edge technology and a deep understanding of the South African automotive market to deliver an experience that works for everyone.

From a first-time buyer searching for an affordable hatchback to a dealership managing hundreds of listings — pikCarz scales to your needs.

Our Mission & Values

Transparency First

Every listing is what it claims to be. We believe buyers deserve complete honesty — from mileage to pricing to seller identity.

Community Driven

pikCarz is built for South Africans, by South Africans. We understand the local market, the culture, and the trust that matters here.

Always Improving

We're constantly evolving — adding features, improving performance, and listening to our users to make pikCarz the best it can be.

Secure & Reliable

Your data, your listings, your transactions — protected at every step. We invest in security so you don't have to think about it.
